About

The first day I started messing with a windows 1995 old PC I decided that this is what I want to do for the rest of my life, at that time I was an only 11-year-old kid who amazed by the amount of data in Encarta. With no internet and no PC, I started learning the basics of the computer in the only training center vacant in my city. It was until 15 that my dad bought us a Pentium 4, since then, I wanted to understand how it really works, I started opening files to see what's inside with no clue, the only thing I remember is that I saw some java code...

I studied computer sciences at the University of Tunis Manar. In 2013, I obtained my master degree in pervasive computing, since then I became a teacher in the College of Ibn Khaldun in Aindraham. The first lesson that a teacher teaches to his students is to never stop learning. For that reason, I wanted to keep myself on the track and update my web development skills with the latest frameworks and tools. I registered on the freecodecamp website as it's free and retains complete training materials. This website made with Bootstrap 4 is part of the training challenges, and here I will keep updating the list of projects that I make.
